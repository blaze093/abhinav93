About Us

Our Story

Blaze was born out of a passion for cybersecurity and ethical hacking. Our founder, Abhinav, started this platform during his B.Com studies, aiming to provide aspiring ethical hackers with the tools and resources they need to succeed. What began as a small idea has now grown into a vibrant community of learners and professionals.

Our Journey

Here's how Blaze evolved over time:

  • 2022: Blaze was founded as a passion project.
  • 2023: Released our first ethical hacking tool.
  • 2024: Bug bounty has been received on more than 10 websites

Our Mission

Our mission is to create a secure digital world by empowering ethical hackers with knowledge, tools, and a supportive community. We believe in innovation, integrity, and collaboration to achieve this goal.

Our Vision

To be the leading platform for ethical hacking and cybersecurity education, inspiring a new generation of security experts to make the internet a safer place for everyone.
